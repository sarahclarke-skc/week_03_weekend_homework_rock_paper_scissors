The Rules

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a game for two or more people.

On the count of three, each player shapes their hand into a fist (rock), a flat hand (paper), or a fist with the middle and index fingers extended and separated, to look like open scissor blades.

To win:

Rock crushes scissors.

Scissors cut paper.

Paper covers rock.
